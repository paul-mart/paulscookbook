Apple Cider Donuts

Ingredients & Instructions:


Prep Time: 2 Hours 20 Minutes
Cooking Time: 20 Minutes
Total Time: 2 hours 40 Minutes
Yield: 8 Donuts
Ingredients:

60g Butter 250g Apple Cider
375g AP Flour 20g Sugar
5g Salt 5g Cinnamon
1g Pumpkin Spice 5g Yeast
Cinnamon & Sugar for dusting 1 Cinnamon Stick

1. Start by browning the butter on medium heat.
2. After 1 minute, add a cinnamon stick to the butter.
3. Continue to cook for another minute and then pour in the apple cider, then turn down the heat to low.
4. After another 1-2 minutes on the heat, pour this mixture to a container.
5. Excluding the dusting ingredients, mix all the dry ingredients in a food processor on medium.
6. Slowly stream in the cider-butter liquid.
7. Flip into oiled medium bowl, rise on counter 2 hours
8. Lightly flour work surface then the dough, Roll Dough into 8x12 rectangle, use ring molds to punch out 6-8 rounds, then the holes.
9. Lay cut donuts onto lightly floured parchment on sheet tray.
10. Press scrap dough back together into rectangle about 1/2" thick and punch 2 more donuts.
11. Proof on counter covered with another sheet tray for 1 hour
12. Fry at 350 for 90 seconds per side. Drain on wire rack, then dust with cinnamon and sugar.
