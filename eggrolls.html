Steak & Cheese Egg Rolls

Ingredients & Instructions:


Prep Time: 15 Minutes
Cooking Time: 30 Minutes
Total Time: 45 Minutes
Yield: 6 egg rolls
Ingredients:

12 oz shaved steak 1/2 cup of Cheez Whiz
6 egg roll wrappers Salt and pepper to taste
Garlic and onion powder to taste (Optional) 1 diced bell pepper
(Optional) 1 diced white onion

1. First season your shaved steak with salt, pepper, onion powder, and garlic powder to taste, then if you are using peppers and onions, sautee them for a few minutes on medium low, then continue do the following. Begin cooking your shaved steak in a pan with oil on medium heat. Cook for about 3 minutes and add your Cheez Whiz, mix well in the pan. Continue cooking for anotheror 3 minutes or until the shaved steak is brown.
2. Take out your egg roll wrappers, then spread a small amount of water with a brush or your fingers along the sides of the egg roll wrapper. Then fill just the middle with you steak and cheese filling and wrap tightly.
3. Once your egg roll wrappers are filled and wrapped, fry them in oil at 350ºF for about 7 minutes, garnish and serve. Enjoy!.
