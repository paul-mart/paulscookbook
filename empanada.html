Beef Empanadas

Ingredients & Instructions:


Prep Time: 20 Minutes
Cooking Time: 1 Hour
Total Time: 1 hour 20 Minutes
Yield: 8 Empanadas
Ingredients:

1 lb ground beef 16 oz sofrito
1 bell pepper diced 1/4 bunch of cilantro
1 cup of monterey cheese 8 empanada disks
1/2 of a yellow nnion (Optional) 1 habenaro
Lime juice of 1 lime Adobo to taste
Salt to taste Black pepper to taste
Sazon to taste Paprika to taste
Garlic powder to taste Cumin to taste

1. Begin to cook the ground beef in a large pan over medium-low heat.
2. When the beef is about 3/4 of the way done, pour the sofrito and seasoning in, mix well.
3. Continue to cook for another few minutes until combined, add cilantro and lime juice if desired.
4. Pre-heat frying oil to 350, fill empanada disks with beef & sofrito mix, topping with cheese. Seal the edges of the disks with your fingers and close with the end of a fork, creating ridges along the side.
5. When the oil is at 350, drop the empanadas in, depending on the spacing of your cooking vessel, drop two at a time.
6. Fry for 7 minutes total, flipping the empanadas halfway through.
7. Take the empanadas out after the 7 minutes, dry off with a paper towel to remove excess oil.
8. Garnish slightly with salt and cilantro, Enjoy!
