Homemade Rustic Garlic Bread

Ingredients & Instructions:


Prep Time: 10 Minutes
Cooking Time: 25 Minutes
Total Time: 35 Minutes
Yield: 1 Loaf
Ingredients:


1 loaf of italian bread 1/2 cups softened butter
4 cloves of garlic 1 tsp of salt
1 tbsp of parsley 1 tbsp of garlic powder
3 tbsp olive oil 3 tbsp parmesan cheese

1. First, finely mince the garlic cloves. Then, add the garlic, garlic powder,butter, parmesan, and oil in a microwave safe dish or in a small
saucepan. Heat this mixture in the microwave for 1 minute or in a small pot over moderate-low heat for 3 minutes.
2. Toast split bread under broiler. Remove bread when it is toasted & golden brown in color. Brush bread liberally with the garlic butter oil mixture. Sprinkle with cheese and parsley. If you added cheese, return to broiler and brown 30 seconds.Cut into chunks and serve.
