Pumpkin Pie


Ingredients & Instructions:


Prep Time: 15 Minutes
Cooking Time: 45 Minutes
Total Time: 50 Minutes
Yield: 1 Pie (9")
Ingredients:

1 can (15 ounce) pumpkin puree 1 large egg
3 egg yolks 14 oz sweetened condensed milk
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg 2.5 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger 1/8 tsp Chinese 5-spice
1/2 teaspoon fine salt 9-inch unbaked pie crust
2 teaspoon sugar 2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
1.5 Cups of Heavy Cream

1. Mix the pumpkin puree, egg yolks, and egg in a large pan.
2. Once combined, add in the sweetened condensed milk and mix until combined again.
3. Add all the spices, then mix.
4. Pour the filling into the unbaked pie crust.
5. Put the pie into a prehated 425 degree oven for 15 minutes
6. After those 15 minutes are up, turn the oven down to 350 and let the pie cook for another 30-35 minutes.
7. Your pie should now be ready, take it out and serve it with a nice dollop of whipped cream.
